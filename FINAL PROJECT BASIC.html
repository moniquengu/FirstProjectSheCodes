About

The Jasmine Dragon is a traditional tea shop in the Upper Ring of Ba Sing Se. Due to its location, its clientele mostly consists of the upper class citizens of the Earth Kingdom capital. It was once a failing business, but was completely revived after Iroh took over. It opened shortly after he and his nephew, Zuko arrived in the city as refugees. Iroh named it the "Jasmine Dragon" because, in his words, the name was "dramatic, poetic", and had a nice ring to it!
